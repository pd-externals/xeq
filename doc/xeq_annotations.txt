Annotations to xeq.pdf

Dealing with time in music:
- using a regular (or irregular) metrical grid
- measure events with a clock and 'warp' the clock

Dealing with structure:
- a static network of onterconnected Pd-objects
- dynamically unfold in successive sequence-rendering messages (which might be sequenced themselves)
- controlling multiple sequence playback objects with another sequence

Simple playback (qlist type):
[read <file>(
 |
[xeq <name>]      [xeq_??? <name>]

Simple playback (pidi type):

[mfread <file>(
 |
[xeq <name>]      [xeq_parse <name>]
